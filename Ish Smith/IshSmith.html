A Journeyman's Travels: Ish Smith's Career Path

Ish Smith isn't the flashiest player. A six-foot-tall point guard with career averages of 7.1 points per game, 2.4 rebounds per game, and 3.8 assists per game, his numbers don't jump off the page. However, Smith's skillset and knowledege of the game makes him an excellent role player, and teams are always happy to have him. In his 14 year career, he's played for 13 different teams, finding ways to contribute every time. Last season, Smith became an NBA Championship for the first time in his career as a member of the Denver Nuggets.
