The slideshow that I made includes last year's division winners in Major League Baseball. Out of all these teams, the Orioles were the biggest suprise: coming into the season, every other team was projected to finish at or near the top of their division except for the Os. However, none of these teams were able to make deep playoff runs. The teams that ended up coming out of the AL and NL were both in the wild card: Arizona and Texas.
